The technology layer

More than matching. A trust, compliance and confidence platform.

Inspired by modern marketplace UX, RideMate adds regulatory-aware workflows, companion verification, pricing transparency and journey auditability.

01

Identity & licence verification

Structured onboarding for learners and Driving Mates, with eligibility status displayed at booking.

02

Time-based pricing engine

Transparent hourly estimates with active-driving and waiting-time logic instead of taxi-style distance fares.

03

Smart journey categories

Errands, test-centre trips, car collection, social outings and confidence drives as productized workflows.

04

Safety check-ins

Start/end confirmation, incident reporting and emergency guidance designed into the booking journey.

05

Compliance guardrails

Declarations, terms and alerts to help prevent misuse and reinforce learner responsibilities.

06

Admin and authority reports

Operational dashboards for verification status, incidents, bookings, cancellations and anonymised impact metrics.

For regulators and partners

A platform built to encourage lawful action, not bypass it.

RideMate is positioned to help learners access eligible accompaniment while clearly separating Driving Mate bookings from formal driving instruction and EDT lessons.

Not a driving school

Standard Mates do not sell formal lessons, EDT or test-pass guarantees.

Not ride-hailing

The learner drives their own insured vehicle; RideMate sells reserved accompaniment time.

Auditable by design

Booking records, verification status and incident workflows support oversight and operational learning.
